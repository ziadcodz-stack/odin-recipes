Green Detox Juice

A simple, refreshing, and healthy juice. This is great for a simpler, more minimalist recipe page.

Description

This vibrant green juice is a fantastic way to get a boost of vitamins and minerals. It's slightly sweet, slightly earthy, and incredibly refreshing. Perfect for a morning kickstart.

Preparation Time: 10 minutes

Servings: 1 large glass

Ingredients

  • 2 large handfuls of fresh spinach
  • 1 medium cucumber
  • 2 green apples, cored
  • 1 lemon, peeled
  • 1-inch piece of fresh ginger

Steps

  1. Wash all produce thoroughly.
  2. If using a juicer, feed all ingredients through the machine according to its instructions.
  3. If using a blender, blend all ingredients on high speed until completely smooth. Pour the mixture through a fine-mesh strainer or a nut milk bag into a pitcher, pressing to extract all the juice.
  4. Pour into a glass, stir, and drink immediately for the best nutritional value.
