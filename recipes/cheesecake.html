Classic New York Cheesecake

A rich, dense, and creamy dessert. Perfect for practicing a more complex, multi-step recipe page.

Description

The ultimate indulgence. This classic baked cheesecake is known for its tall stature, velvety smooth texture, and a simple sour cream topping. It's a showstopper that requires patience but is worth every minute.

🕑 Preparation Time: 30 minutes

🥣 Cooking Time: 1 hour 30 minutes (plus several hours of chilling)

🍽️ Servings: 12

Ingredients

For the Crust:

  • 1 ½ cups graham cracker crumbs
  • ¼ cup white sugar
  • 6 tablespoons melted butter

For the Filling:

  • 4 (8 ounce) packages full-fat cream cheese, softened
  • 1 ½ cups white sugar
  • ¾ cup whole milk
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 1 tablespoon vanilla extract
  • ¼ cup all-purpose flour

For the Topping:

  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Mix graham cracker crumbs, ¼ cup sugar, and melted butter. Press firmly onto the bottom of a 9-inch springform pan.
  3. Bake the crust for 10 minutes. Remove and let it cool. Reduce oven temperature to 325°F (165°C).
  4. In a large bowl, beat the cream cheese and 1 ½ cups sugar until smooth. Blend in the milk, then mix in the eggs one at a time. Finally, mix in the sour cream, vanilla, and flour until just combined. Pour over the prepared crust.
  5. Bake in the preheated oven for about 1 hour and 15 minutes, or until the center is almost set.
  6. While the cheesecake is baking, mix the topping ingredients: 1 cup sour cream, 2 tablespoons sugar, and 1 teaspoon vanilla.
  7. Remove the cheesecake from the oven. Gently spread the sour cream topping over the hot surface. Return to the oven for 5 more minutes.
  8. Turn off the oven, crack the door open, and let the cheesecake cool inside for 1 hour to prevent cracking.
  9. Chill in the refrigerator for at least 4 hours, or preferably overnight, before serving.
