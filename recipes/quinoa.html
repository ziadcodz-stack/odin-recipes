Quinoa Power Bowl with Lemon-Tahini Dressing

A vibrant, healthy, and filling meal that's perfect for lunch or dinner. You can practice listing customizable ingredients.

Description

A nutrient-packed bowl that is completely customizable. It's a perfect make-ahead meal for the week. The star is the creamy, tangy lemon-tahini dressing.

Ingredients

For the Bowl:

  • 1 cup cooked quinoa
  • 1 can (15 oz) chickpeas, rinsed and drained
  • 1 teaspoon olive oil
  • ¬Ω teaspoon paprika
  • 1 avocado, sliced
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 cucumber, diced
  • 2 large handfuls of fresh spinach or kale

For the Lemon-Tahini Dressing:

  • ¬º cup tahini
  • 3 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 2 tablespoons water (plus more to thin)
  • 1 small clove garlic, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Cook quinoa according to package instructions and let it cool slightly.
  2. In a small bowl, toss the chickpeas with olive oil and paprika.
  3. To make the dressing, whisk together the tahini, lemon juice, water, and minced garlic in a small bowl. It will thicken at first, so keep whisking and add a little more water until it reaches a creamy, pourable consistency. Season with salt and pepper.
  4. To assemble the bowls, divide the spinach between two bowls. Top with a generous scoop of quinoa, the spiced chickpeas, avocado slices, cherry tomatoes, and cucumber.
  5. Drizzle generously with the lemon-tahini dressing and serve immediately.
